## Operating rules

The automated 10-post cycle guarantees the requested 40/30/20/10 distribution. Every generated post contains a hook, value, three CTAs, and local hashtags. Reels place the mandatory follow sentence after the hashtag block so that it is always the final sentence.

Before posting a real student success story, confirm the student's name spelling, course/result, permission to publish, and supporting proof. The default generator deliberately uses anonymous progress-oriented copy rather than fabricating results.
